Simplex

Seeded simplex noise. Cheaper than Perlin in higher dimensions and free of its axis-aligned artifacts. Output is approximately [-1, 1]. 

local simplex = Atlas.Noise.Simplex.new(42)
local density = simplex:sample3D(x * 0.05, y * 0.05, z * 0.05)
